Boba Paw



Flavors: ^Grape-Boba ^Mango-Boba Grapefruit-Boba^ Orange-Boba^






Boba Paw is a fun and cozy boba tea shop in Winnipeg that's known for its cute vibe and delicious drinks. Their bubble tea is super refreshing, with chewy tapioca pearls and unique flavors you won't find anywhere else. They also serve yummy snacks like mochi donuts that go perfectly with your drink. Everything is made fresh, and you can tell they care about quality and taste. If you love sweet treats and trying something new, Boba Paw is the perfect spot to grab your next favorite drink!
